mt:categoryisactive
A conditional tag that will render its contents if the category in context matches either the archive category or the category of the entry in context.

mt:categoryancestorisactive
A conditional tag that will render its contents if the category in context is an ancestor of either the archive category or the category of the entry in context.

mt:categorydescendantisactive
A conditional tag that will render its contents if the category in context is a descendant of either the archive category or the category of the entry in context.
